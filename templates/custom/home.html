Seagull is my most complete Zola theme

The author

Seagull is a Zola theme designed for complete websites. It has a lot of features and a lot of customization options.

The theme was designed in parallel on Scientifiques en Rebellion website and La BASE Toulouse website. It was then refactored into a single theme to make maintainance easier.
